## Introduction

Stage is a mobile robots simulator that provides a 2D environment for simulating multiple robots. It is widely used in robotics research and development due to its simplicity and efficiency. In this blog post, we will explore the features of Stage, its applications, and how it can be integrated with the Robot Operating System (ROS) for enhanced robotic simulations.
